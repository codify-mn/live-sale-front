# Products

The Products module allows you to manage your entire product catalog including variants, pricing, and inventory.

## Product Management

### Creating Products

To create a new product:

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** > **Products**
2. Click **Add Product**
3. Fill in the required information

### Product Fields

| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Name | Product display name | Yes |
| SKU | Unique product code | Yes |
| Description | Product details | No |
| Price | Selling price | Yes |
| Category | Product category | No |

## Variants

Products can have multiple variants (e.g., different sizes or colors).

### Adding Variants

1. Open a product
2. Click **Add Variant**
3. Specify the variant options:
   - Name (e.g., "Red - Large")
   - SKU
   - Price (if different from base)
   - Stock quantity

## Inventory

### Stock Tracking

Singulatim automatically tracks inventory:

- Stock decreases when orders are placed
- Stock increases when orders are cancelled
- Low stock alerts notify you when items need restocking

### Low Stock Alerts

Configure alerts in **Settings** > **Notifications**:

- Set minimum stock thresholds
- Enable email or dashboard notifications
- View all low-stock items at a glance
