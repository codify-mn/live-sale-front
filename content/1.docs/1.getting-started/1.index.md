# Introduction

Comment Boost is a powerful platform designed to streamline your live commerce business. Whether you're selling on Facebook Live or other platforms, we provide the tools you need to succeed.

## What is Comment Boost?

Comment Boost helps you:

- **Process orders in real-time** during live streams
- **Manage your inventory** with ease
- **Track customer interactions** and purchase history
- **Analyze your sales** with detailed reports

## Key Features

::list
- Real-time order processing
- Product and inventory management
- Customer relationship management
- Facebook Live integration
- Analytics and reporting
::
