# Installation

This guide will walk you through setting up Singulatim for your business.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Facebook account with admin access to your business page
- A business email address
- Basic information about your products

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [Sign Up](/signup)
2. Enter your name, email, and create a password
3. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Connect Facebook

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** > **Settings**
2. Click **Connect Facebook**
3. Authorize Singulatim to access your page
4. Select the Facebook page you want to use

## Step 3: Add Your Products

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Products**
2. Click **Add Product**
3. Fill in the product details:
   - Name
   - Description
   - Price
   - Stock quantity
   - Images

## Next Steps

Once you've completed the setup, check out our [Usage Guide](/docs/getting-started/usage) to learn how to run your first live sale.
